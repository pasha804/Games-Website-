Assassin’s Creed: Shadows Download

Assassin’s Creed: Shadows Direct Download:

The Black Samurai In Assassin’s Creed: Shadows we actually play as a real character for the first time in the franchise as we play as Yasuke who is known as The Black Samurai, there is actually an awesome anime based on him on Netflix that is well worth watching. What I like about Yasuke is that he feels more like what we had in Assassin’s Creed: Valhalla where he is a straight up fighter with some heavy duty big ass weapons that he can use to tackle foes head on. There are some great games in 2024, but I do not think I am more excited to play as anyone than I am Yasuke

Sticking To The Shadows Of course the “gimmick” of Assassin’s Creed: Shadows is that we have two playable characters. The other character we can play as is a shinobi called Naoe and she plays more like a classic Assassin’s Creed character where she is quick, nimble and all about stealthily taking down her enemies. She has this grappling hook that can be used in combat, but it is how this is used to traverse the game that looks so much fun. One thing we do not know yet is how we will be playing as the characters. Will certain sections be locked to certain characters?
